The Price is Right Instant Win

Come on down! It’s your big chance to win as you play through up 4 different types of game. Match the symbols to win prizes and you might be lucky enough to also play 1 or 2 other bonus games. Can you make it to the top of the mountain without falling off in Cliff Hangers? How big will your multiplier be after spinning the wheel in Showcase Showdown? It could be your lucky night!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

The object of The Price is Right Instant Win is to reveal all the symbols on the base game. The base game is made up of four games; two rows in Game 1 and two rows in Game 2.

In Game 1; if all three revealed prizes match then that prize is won.

In Game 2; if any of the Your Symbols matches the Winning Symbol in that row then the at-tached prize is won.

During the base game bonus icons can be revealed. If the Cliff Hangers meter is completed by revealing 3 icons then the player will be playing Cliff Hangers upon completing the base game. If the Showcase Showdown icon is revealed then the player will be taken to play Showcase Showdown at the end of the game.

In Cliff Hangers; the spinning reel of numbers is stopped when the attached button is pressed. The Yodeller will climb this number of stops up the mountain. If he stops in a prize section that prize value is won. The reel starts spinning again and continues to be stopped until the Yodeller either stops at the top of the mountain or falls off it. All wins collected dur-ing the climb are totalled up and awarded to the player.

In Showcase Showdown the Big Wheel is covered in multipliers. The wheel is spun and whichever multiplier it lands on is applied to the total win.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. The base game results are shown instantly and any bonuses are played through without player interaction.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
